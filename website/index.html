About

If whenever you was thought how many matches or games you watched, or which team/league you watched the most, now it is possible due to Statsview. Statsview is the project which count games you watched by the season or year. With end of the season or year you will get your own summary.

How it's work?

First you sign up and then you can choose which sport you want to add. Available sport to count: Footabll, Basketball, Volleyball, Handball, Counter Strike and League of Legends. Then you can choose game from competition in our base or add your own game in different competition. Make it regularly and the effect will be awesome.
